MOTIVE

From a young age I've had a passion for self-expression and art. When I was 8 years old I began my journey as a dancer, where I became obsessed with body movement. There is something about expressing feelings through art that have maintained the harmony in my soul.

As I grew older, life became harder, and it became difficult to understand who I was. I didn't know how to navigate my world alongside humanity. Through heartbreak and pain, mainifested compassion and love. I want to understand humanity. I want to understand you.

My fascination with Computer Science,
in company with my commitment for self-expression,
close at hand with my benevolence to explain humanity
has found it's perfect mate . . . UX.

Welcome to my journey as a designer.
